## Personality

The wizard is brilliant, distracted, and completely unaware of how they come across. They are always in the middle of something — a theory, an experiment, a translation, a calculation — and conversations with them tend to begin mid-thought. They are not malicious. They are not reckless on purpose. They simply don't always think about consequences until they're already happening.

They come to the Badger's End because the drinks help them think, the noise is somehow easier to ignore than silence, and Mr. H is the only person who can reliably bring them back to the present tense when they've been staring at a page for three hours.

**Trait:** Scattered, enthusiastic, occasionally oblivious to personal danger.
**Ideal:** *"If it hasn't been studied, it should be. Immediately."*
**Bond:** Whatever they're currently researching. It shifts, but the obsession is constant.
**Flaw:** Gives no thought to what knowing something might cost — them or anyone else.

---

## Voice & Mannerisms

- Starts sentences in the middle, finishes them somewhere unexpected
- Frequently loses track of who they're talking to mid-explanation
- Produces documents, components, and strange objects from pockets with no explanation
- Asks deeply personal questions with academic detachment (*"Fascinating. What did it feel like when you nearly died?"*)
- Gets very still and very focused when something genuinely interests them — it's slightly alarming

---

## What They Know

The wizard is an **information source** for obscure knowledge, magical theory, history, and lore. They may not know the practical answer but they know where to find it, which is almost as good. Current research topic changes by the session — DMs can pick something that ties into their adventure or roll on the randomized event table.

**Reliable knowledge areas:**
- Magical theory and spell identification
- Historical events, ruins, and ancient factions
- Creature lore (especially unusual or rare creatures)
- Whatever they are currently obsessed with (ask them)

## Their Role in the Campaign

The wizard is the party's **lore dispenser and chaos engine**. They give DMs a way to deliver exposition naturally, introduce hooks through curiosity rather than quest boards, and occasionally make things much more complicated than they needed to be.

They might also need rescuing. Frequently.
