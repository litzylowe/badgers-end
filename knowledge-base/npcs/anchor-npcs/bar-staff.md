# Bar Staff & Kitchen — The Badger's End

The Badger's End runs on more than Mr. H. These are the people who keep the drinks flowing, the food coming, and the place standing. They're familiar faces — not quite anchor NPCs in the full sense, but present enough that players notice when they're missing.

## Staff Dynamics

- Mr. H is clearly in charge but doesn't manage through authority — he just is, and everyone follows
- The head chef and Mr. H have an easy understanding; the kitchen is theirs, the bar is his
- The junior barkeep looks up to the senior barkeeper and is slightly afraid of the late night one
- Mr. Hoots is accepted by all staff as simply part of the establishment. Nobody questions it

---

## Staff as Scene Tools

The bar staff exist to make the Badger's End feel inhabited. DMs should use them to:
- Deliver small pieces of information or gossip in passing
- React to strange events so the party can see how "normal" people respond
- Create texture — a busy night feels different from a quiet one when there are named faces moving through it
- Occasionally become the center of a problem themselves
