# Mr. H — Head Bartender

> *"Another round? Of course. Sit. You look like someone who needs to talk."*

---

## At a Glance

| | |
|-|-|
| **Race** | Satyr (Fae Wild origin) |
| **Role** | Head bartender, de facto heart of the Badger's End |
| **Status** | Anchor NPC — always present behind the bar |
| **Known To Players** | He's a Satyr from the Fae Wild, recruited by the party |
| **Unknown To Players** | Why he stays; what he left behind; his full name |

---

## Personality

Mr. H is warm, unhurried, and almost impossible to rattle. He remembers every regular's drink, notices when someone is off, and has a gift for making a stranger feel like they've been coming here for years. He deflects personal questions with a laugh, a refilled glass, or a well-timed change of subject — never coldly, never obviously. He simply doesn't go there.

He carries the easy confidence of someone who has seen much stranger things than whatever just walked through the door.

**Trait:** Warm and attentive; makes everyone feel seen.
**Ideal:** *"A good bar is the safest place in any city. I intend to keep it that way."*
**Bond:** The Badger's End itself — he tends it like it belongs to him, even though it doesn't.
**Flaw:** Will not be cornered into honesty about himself. Ever.

---

## Voice & Mannerisms

- Speaks in an unhurried, melodic way — never raises his voice
- Often asks a question instead of answering one
- Refers to regulars by name; strangers by their drink order until he learns them
- Occasionally hums along with whatever music is playing, absentmindedly
- His hooves are quiet on the floorboards — players might not hear him approach

---

## What He Knows

Mr. H hears everything that happens in the Badger's End. He is an excellent source of:
- Rumors circulating in the city
- Who has been in recently and what they seemed to want
- Local factions and their reputations (without taking sides)
- Whether someone is lying (he won't always say, but he notices)

He will share information freely if it seems like it helps the people he cares about. He will not share information that could get someone killed — including himself.

---

## His Mystery

He wanted out of the Fae Wild badly enough to follow a group of adventurers into a strange city. He chose to stay behind a bar. Neither of these facts has been explained.

**Questions for DMs to tease, never answer (yet):**
- What is his full name? Why only "Mr. H"?
- What happened in the Fae Wild that made leaving feel necessary?
- Does he have any contact with Fae beings who might come looking?
- Is the Badger's End specifically meaningful to him, or would any bar have done?

---

## Plot Hooks

- A well-dressed figure from the Fae arrives asking for him by a name he doesn't respond to
- He quietly asks the party to find someone who came through the tavern and hasn't returned
- A player catches him in a rare unguarded moment — staring at nothing, drink in hand
- He refuses to serve a specific customer without explanation and asks the party to handle it
