# Classes Overview

| Class | Hit Die | Spellcasting | Armor | Primary Ability |
|-------|---------|-------------|-------|----------------|
| Barbarian | d12 | None | Light, Medium, Shields | STR |
| Bard | d8 | Full (CHA) | Light | CHA |
| Cleric | d8 | Full (WIS) | Light, Medium, Shields | WIS |
| Druid | d8 | Full (WIS) | Light, Medium, Shields (no metal) | WIS |
| Fighter | d10 | None (EK: partial INT) | All, Shields | STR or DEX |
| Monk | d8 | None | None | DEX + WIS |

| Ranger | d10 | Half (WIS) | Light, Medium, Shields | DEX + WIS |

## Class Summaries

### Barbarian
- **Role:** Frontline melee bruiser.
- **Key Feature — Rage:** Bonus damage on STR attacks, resistance to physical damage, can't cast concentration spells. Limited uses per long rest.
- **Key Feature — Unarmored Defense:** AC = 10 + DEX mod + CON mod (no armor).
- **Primal Path (level 3):** Berserker, Totem Warrior, and others.

### Bard
- **Role:** Support, face, and versatile spellcaster.
- **Key Feature — Bardic Inspiration:** Give a d6 (scaling) to an ally for attack rolls, ability checks, or saving throws.
- **Key Feature — Jack of All Trades:** Add half proficiency to all skills you're not proficient in.
- **Bard College (level 3):** Lore, Valor, Glamour, Swords, and others.
- **Spells Known:** Fixed number; CHA-based.

### Cleric
- **Role:** Healer, support, and divine spellcaster. Can also be a melee combatant.
- **Key Feature — Divine Domain (level 1):** Chooses at creation; grants bonus spells (always prepared) and features. (Life, Light, Trickery, War, Knowledge, Nature, Tempest, etc.)
- **Spells:** WIS-based; prepares spells from full list each day.
- **Channel Divinity:** Class-specific powers; 1/rest (more later).

### Druid
- **Role:** Nature magic, shapeshifting, versatile.
- **Key Feature — Wild Shape:** Transform into beasts. CR limited by level.
- **Druid Circle (level 2):** Land, Moon, Spores, Stars, and others.
- **Spells:** WIS-based; prepares from full list each day. Can't use metal armor or shields.

### Fighter
- **Role:** Durable, versatile martial combatant.
- **Key Feature — Fighting Style:** +2 to a specific attack type, or other bonuses.
- **Key Feature — Second Wind:** Bonus action, heal 1d10 + Fighter level. 1/short rest.
- **Key Feature — Action Surge:** Take one additional action on your turn. 1/short rest (2 at level 17).
- **Key Feature — Extra Attack:** 2 attacks at level 5, 3 at level 11, 4 at level 20.
- **Martial Archetype (level 3):** Champion, Battle Master, Eldritch Knight, and others.

### Monk
- **Role:** Mobile, unarmored melee combatant with ki powers.
- **Key Feature — Martial Arts:** DEX for unarmed/monk weapons; bonus action unarmed strike.
- **Key Feature — Ki:** Pool of points for special abilities (Flurry of Blows, Patient Defense, Step of the Wind).
- **Key Feature — Unarmored Movement:** Speed bonus; eventually can run on walls/water.
- **Monastic Tradition (level 3):** Open Hand, Shadow, Four Elements, and others.

### Paladin
- **Role:** Holy warrior — melee fighter + healer/support caster.
- **Key Feature — Divine Smite:** Expend a spell slot after a hit to deal extra radiant damage (2d8 per slot level, more vs undead/fiends).
- **Key Feature — Lay on Hands:** Pool of HP equal to Paladin level × 5. Heal or cure disease/poison.
- **Key Feature — Aura of Protection (level 5):** Allies within 10 ft. add CHA modifier to saving throws.
- **Sacred Oath (level 3):** Devotion, Ancients, Vengeance, Oathbreaker, and others.

### Ranger
- **Role:** Wilderness expert and skirmisher.
- **Key Feature — Favored Enemy:** Advantage on Survival to track and on INT checks about chosen enemy type.
- **Key Feature — Natural Explorer:** Doubled proficiency on INT/WIS checks in favored terrain; travel benefits.
- **Key Feature — Extra Attack (level 5):** Two attacks per Attack action.
- **Ranger Conclave (level 3):** Hunter, Beast Master, Gloom Stalker, and others.

### Rogue
- **Role:** Skill expert, precision attacker, and infiltrator.
- **Key Feature — Sneak Attack:** Once per turn, +damage (d6 per 2 rogue levels) when you have advantage OR an ally is adjacent to the target and you don't have disadvantage.
- **Key Feature — Expertise:** Double proficiency on 2 chosen skills (more at level 6).
- **Key Feature — Uncanny Dodge / Evasion:** Halve damage from attacks; avoid AoE damage on success.
- **Roguish Archetype (level 3):** Thief, Assassin, Arcane Trickster, Inquisitive, and others.

### Sorcerer
- **Role:** Innate arcane spellcaster with flexible spell shaping.
- **Key Feature — Sorcery Points:** Fuel metamagic (Twinned, Quickened, Subtle, Empowered, etc.) and convert to/from spell slots.
- **Spells Known:** Fixed list; CHA-based.
- **Sorcerous Origin (level 1):** Draconic Bloodline, Wild Magic, Divine Soul, Shadow Magic, and others.

### Warlock
- **Role:** Powerful short-rest caster with unique invocation abilities.
- **Key Feature — Eldritch Blast:** Default cantrip (force damage, scales with level); customized by invocations.
- **Key Feature — Pact Magic:** Few but high-level slots; recover on short rest.
- **Key Feature — Eldritch Invocations:** Permanent ability buffs (e.g., see in magical darkness, cast Mage Armor at will).
- **Otherworldly Patron (level 1):** Fiend, Great Old One, Archfey, Celestial, Hexblade, and others.
- **Pact Boon (level 3):** Blade, Chain, or Tome.

### Wizard
- **Role:** Broadest and most flexible arcane spellcaster.
- **Key Feature — Spellbook:** Learn spells by scribing; prepare a subset each day (INT-based).
- **Key Feature — Arcane Recovery:** Recover spell slots (up to half wizard level total) on short rest.
- **Key Feature — Ritual Casting:** Cast any ritual spell from spellbook without preparing it.
- **Arcane Tradition (level 2):** Evocation, Divination, Conjuration, Abjuration, Necromancy, Illusion, Transmutation, Enchantment, Bladesinging, War Magic, and others.

## Key Milestone Levels

| Level | Universal |
|-------|-----------|
| 1 | Starting level; racial features; class features |
| 3 | Subclass chosen for most classes |
| 4 | Ability Score Improvement (ASI) — +2 to one ability, or +1 to two, or a Feat |
| 5 | Extra Attack (martials); 3rd-level spell slots; cantrip damage increases |
| 8 | Second ASI |
| 11 | 6th-level spell slots; cantrip damage increases |
| 12 | Third ASI |
| 17 | 9th-level spell slots; cantrip damage increases |
| 19 | Fifth ASI |
| 20 | Capstone class feature |
