## Third Casters
*(Arcane Trickster Rogue, Eldritch Knight Fighter — spells begin at class level 3)*

These use the full caster table but delayed by two-thirds:
- Their "spellcasting level" = class level ÷ 3 (round down).
- Max spell level is 4th.

---

## Multiclass Spellcasting

When multiclassing, combine caster levels to determine total spell slots:

| Class | Contribution |
|-------|-------------|
| Full caster | Full class level |
| Half caster (Paladin, Ranger) | Half class level (round down) |
| Third caster (AT Rogue, EK Fighter) | Third class level (round down) |
| Warlock | Uses Pact Magic separately — not combined |

Use the **combined level** on the full caster table to find total spell slots.

*Example: Fighter 3 (EK) + Wizard 5 = 1 + 5 = 6th level caster → use row 6 on the full caster table.*
