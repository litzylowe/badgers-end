# Movement & Travel

## Movement in Combat

- Each creature has a **speed** (feet per turn) on its stat block or character sheet.
- You can **split movement** before and after actions.
- Moving through **difficult terrain** costs 2 feet per foot of movement.
- You can move through an ally's space but not end your turn there.
- Moving through a **hostile creature's space** requires the creature to be at least two sizes larger or smaller than you.

### Movement Costs

| Action | Cost |
|--------|------|
| Move normally | 1 ft. per ft. |
| Difficult terrain | 2 ft. per ft. |
| Crawling (prone) | 2 ft. per ft. |
| Climbing | 2 ft. per ft. (unless you have a climb speed) |
| Swimming | 2 ft. per ft. (unless you have a swim speed) |
| Long jump (running) | STR score in feet (requires 10 ft. run-up) |
| Long jump (standing) | Half STR score in feet |
| High jump (running) | 3 + STR modifier feet (requires 10 ft. run-up) |
| High jump (standing) | Half the above |

### Dropping Prone & Standing Up
- **Dropping prone:** Free (0 movement cost).
- **Standing up:** Costs half your movement speed.

## Overland Travel

### Travel Pace

| Pace | Miles/Day | Miles/Hour | Effect |
|------|-----------|------------|--------|
| **Fast** | 30 | 4 | −5 penalty to Passive Perception |
| **Normal** | 24 | 3 | — |
| **Slow** | 18 | 2 | Can use stealth; can forage |

*(Assumes 8 hours of travel per day.)*

### Difficult Terrain Overland
Halve the miles-per-day if traveling through:
- Dense forest, swamps, mountains, deep snow, or similar terrain.

---

## Falling

- Fall **500 feet** per round (at the end of your turn).
- Take **1d6 bludgeoning damage per 10 feet fallen**, max **20d6**.
- Fall **prone** at the end of the fall unless you avoid the damage.
- A creature that catches you or that you land on may also take damage (DM's discretion).

---

## Special Movement Types

### Flying
- Must end turn on a surface or continue flying or fall.
- Prone while flying causes a fall unless the creature can hover.

### Swimming
- Requires a Strength (Athletics) check in rough water.
- Creatures without a swim speed treat water as difficult terrain.

### Climbing
- Requires Athletics checks for difficult surfaces (DM's discretion).
- Creatures without a climb speed treat climbing as difficult terrain.

## Visibility & Light

| Condition | Effect |
|-----------|--------|
| **Bright Light** | Normal vision |
| **Dim Light** | Lightly obscured — disadvantage on Perception checks relying on sight |
| **Darkness** | Heavily obscured — effectively blind (disadvantage on attacks, attacks against have advantage) |

### Darkvision
- Can see in darkness as if it were dim light (no color, only grayscale).
- **Does not** let you see in magical darkness.

### Blindsight
- Perceives surroundings without relying on sight, within a radius.

### Tremorsense
- Detects vibrations through contact with ground/surfaces.

### Truesight
- Sees in normal and magical darkness, sees invisible creatures, sees through illusions and transmutations, sees true form of shapechangers and polymorph effects, and perceives the Ethereal Plane.

---

## Forced March

After 8 hours of travel, each additional hour requires a **DC 10 + 1 per extra hour Constitution saving throw**. On a fail, gain one level of **exhaustion**.

## Encumbrance (Optional Rule)

| Condition | Weight Carried |
|-----------|---------------|
| Carrying capacity | STR × 15 lbs. |
| Encumbered | STR × 5 lbs. — speed −10 ft. |
| Heavily encumbered | STR × 10 lbs. — speed −20 ft., disadvantage on STR/DEX/CON checks and saves |

*(Standard variant: just track whether it feels reasonable.)*
