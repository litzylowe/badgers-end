# Conditions Reference

Conditions are applied by spells, abilities, and environmental effects. They end when their source specifies, or when cured (e.g., by *lesser restoration*).

---

## All Conditions

### Blinded
- Can't see; automatically fails checks requiring sight.
- Attack rolls against the creature have **advantage**.
- The creature's attack rolls have **disadvantage**.

### Charmed
- Can't attack the charmer or target them with harmful abilities or magic.
- The charmer has **advantage** on social interaction checks with the creature.

### Deafened
- Can't hear; automatically fails checks requiring hearing.

### Exhaustion
Exhaustion is measured in six levels. Multiple effects stack.

| Level | Effect |
|-------|--------|
| 1 | Disadvantage on ability checks |
| 2 | Speed halved |
| 3 | Disadvantage on attack rolls and saving throws |
| 4 | Hit point maximum halved |
| 5 | Speed reduced to 0 |
| 6 | Death |

Finishing a long rest removes **one level** of exhaustion (requires food and water).

### Frightened
- **Disadvantage** on ability checks and attack rolls while the source of fear is in line of sight.
- Can't **willingly** move closer to the source of fear.

### Grappled
- Speed becomes **0** and can't benefit from bonuses to speed.
- Ends if the grappler is incapacitated or if an effect removes the creature from reach.

### Incapacitated
- **Can't take actions or reactions.**
- (Note: this is a component of several other conditions.)

### Invisible
- Impossible to see without special sense.
- Attack rolls against the creature have **disadvantage**.
- The creature's attack rolls have **advantage**.
- The creature can still be detected by noise, footprints, etc.

### Paralyzed
- Incapacitated; can't move or speak.
- Automatically fails Strength and Dexterity saving throws.
- Attack rolls against the creature have **advantage**.
- Any attack from within 5 ft. that hits is a **critical hit**.

### Petrified
- Transformed into solid inanimate substance.
- Incapacitated; can't move, speak, or be aware of surroundings.
- Attack rolls against the creature have **advantage**.
- Automatically fails Strength and Dexterity saving throws.
- Resistance to all damage; immune to poison and disease (existing effects suspended, not cured).

### Poisoned
- **Disadvantage** on attack rolls and ability checks.

### Prone
- Can only **crawl** (costs 1 extra foot of movement per foot traveled) or stand up (costs half your speed).
- **Disadvantage** on attack rolls.
- Attack rolls against the creature: **advantage** if attacker is within 5 ft., otherwise **disadvantage**.

### Restrained
- Speed becomes **0** and can't benefit from bonuses to speed.
- Attack rolls against the creature have **advantage**.
- The creature's attack rolls have **disadvantage**.
- **Disadvantage** on Dexterity saving throws.

### Stunned
- Incapacitated; can't move; can only speak falteringly.
- Automatically fails Strength and Dexterity saving throws.
- Attack rolls against the creature have **advantage**.

### Unconscious
- Incapacitated; can't move or speak; unaware of surroundings.
- Drops whatever it's holding and falls prone.
- Automatically fails Strength and Dexterity saving throws.
- Attack rolls against the creature have **advantage**.
- Any attack from within 5 ft. that hits is a **critical hit**.

## Quick Reference: Advantage/Disadvantage on Attacks

| Condition | Attacks vs. Creature | Creature's Attacks |
|-----------|---------------------|--------------------|
| Blinded | Advantage | Disadvantage |
| Invisible | Disadvantage | Advantage |
| Paralyzed | Advantage + crit within 5 ft. | — |
| Petrified | Advantage | — |
| Prone | Advantage (≤5 ft.) / Disadvantage (>5 ft.) | Disadvantage |
| Restrained | Advantage | Disadvantage |
| Stunned | Advantage | — |
| Unconscious | Advantage + crit within 5 ft. | — |

## Curing Conditions

| Spell/Ability | Removes |
|---------------|---------|
| *Lesser Restoration* | Blinded, Deafened, Paralyzed, Poisoned, one disease |
| *Greater Restoration* | Charmed, Exhaustion (all), Frightened, Paralyzed, Petrified, Cursed |
| *Remove Curse* | Cursed |
| *Calm Emotions* | Charmed, Frightened (temporarily) |
| *Freedom of Movement* | Grappled, Paralyzed, Restrained (by nonmagical means) |
